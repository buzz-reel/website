Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how BuzzReel ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application. By using BuzzReel, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care.

Definitions

  • Account — A unique profile created when you register with our service.
  • Application — BuzzReel, the iOS software program provided by us.
  • Device — Any electronic device capable of accessing the App, including iPhones and iPads.
  • Personal Data — Information that can be used to identify an individual.
  • Usage Data — Data collected automatically during your interaction with the App.
  • Service Provider — A third-party entity that processes data on our behalf.
  • You / User — The individual accessing or using the App.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

When you use BuzzReel, we may collect certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your email address, display name, and usage data.

Usage Data

We automatically collect usage data when you interact with the App. This may include:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Pages and content you view within the App
  • Time, date, and duration of your sessions
  • App crash reports and performance diagnostics

Information Collected During Use

With your permission, we may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Content interaction and viewing history
  • Search queries within the App
  • App engagement patterns

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery — To provide, maintain, and improve BuzzReel's core features and content.
  • Account Management — To manage your registration, preferences, and viewing history.
  • Personalization — To recommend content based on your viewing habits and preferences.
  • Communication — To send you updates, new episode alerts, and service announcements.
  • Analytics — To understand usage trends and improve the overall user experience.
  • Security — To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent activity.

Data Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers — With trusted partners who assist in operating and improving our App.
  • Business Transfers — In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales.
  • Legal Requirements — When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • With Your Consent — For any other purpose with your explicit agreement.
Important: We will never sell or rent your personal contact information (such as email or phone number) to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Advertising

BuzzReel may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks. These partners may collect limited data to deliver relevant ads:

Data Shared with Ad Partners

  • Advertising identifiers (resettable through device settings)
  • Basic device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Session frequency and engagement duration
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • Non-specific demographic information (country, language)

Data NOT Shared with Ad Partners

  • Personal contact information
  • Account credentials
  • Specific viewing history or progress
  • Precise geolocation data

Our Application Server

BuzzReel operates its own servers to provide core functionality. Data transmitted to our servers is used exclusively to:

  • Deliver streaming content and the core viewing experience
  • Synchronize viewing progress and preferences across sessions
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Deliver content updates
  • Respond to support requests
Security: All data transfers use TLS 1.2+ encryption. We implement strict access controls, apply data minimization principles, and conduct regular security assessments.

Data Retention

We retain your Personal Data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. If you do not access or use BuzzReel for a continuous period of 90 days, we will delete your Personal Data from our systems.

Usage Data may be retained for internal analysis purposes in anonymized or aggregated form that cannot be used to identify you.

Data Transfer

Your information may be transferred to and processed on servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

All Users

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability — Request your data in a portable format.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, and shared.
  • Right to delete personal information.
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

EEA Residents (GDPR)

  • Right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data.
  • Right to restrict or object to processing.
  • Right to data portability.

Virginia Residents (VCDPA)

  • Right to access, correct, and delete your personal data.
  • Right to data portability.
  • Right to opt-out of targeted advertising, data sale, and profiling.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at buzz_reel@outlook.com.

Opting Out

Targeted Advertising

You can manage ad personalization through your device settings:

  • iOS — Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Data Sale

If you reside in California or Virginia, you may opt-out by emailing us with the subject line "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" at buzz_reel@outlook.com.

Children's Privacy

BuzzReel is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their policies.

App Permissions

BuzzReel requests the following permissions on your device:

  • Internet Access — Required for streaming content and loading advertisements.
  • Network State — Used to optimize playback quality based on connection type.
  • Advertising ID (IDFA) — Used for personalized advertising. You can reset or limit this through iOS settings.
  • Notifications — Used to alert you about new episodes and updates (opt-in only).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top and notify you through the App or via email for significant changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out:

  • Email: buzz_reel@outlook.com
  • In-App: Go to Settings > Help & Support

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours.
